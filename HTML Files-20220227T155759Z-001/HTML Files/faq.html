FAQs

Does BookEx BMSCE deal with financial transaction b/w users?

No, the website only displays the available sellers and the prices they offer. Users upon mutual agreement decide on a payment method and complete the transaction when they meet at a common area.

Where can I view the status of the book I chose?

Details of the book selected such as selected book,price, payment method and area of meet can be found in the cart section of the frontpage menubar.

Can users buy/sell books without an account?

Users would be required to create an account to buy their desired books or upload a book for sale. However, they can view the books and their sellers without creating an account.

Are prices of books fixed?

The price displayed for a certain book is decided by the seller. This cannot be changed , unless the user agrees to sell it for a different price after discussion.

What to do after a transaction is complete?

The user and seller would have to declare a transaction complete after which that book from that seller would be removed from list wher buyers usual see the different sell options.
